Who's Pratik

Pratik's main area of Legal Practice being Criminal Matters, Matrimonial Suits with special interest in Cyber Law, although his Practice range stretches a little to Civil Matters, including Public Interest Litigations, Company Matters, etc. He Graduated from Chandigarh University with a B.COM LLB Degree, then acquiring the Licence of the Bar Council of India to start Legal Practice. In his young professional journey, he has the opportunity to appear before the Hon'ble High Court and district courts along with some renowned Senior Counsels as a Junior and has also appeared successfully as Counsel in quite a few notable Matters, some of which are reported. He is known to be selective of his cases and always stresses on developing a healthy professional relationship with his client, to be able to cater to specific needs in particular. His sheer perseverance and dedication towards his profession and his clients, make him stand out from many. Money always comes second to professional satisfaction, according to him and this is what defines his passion for the legal profession aptly.
